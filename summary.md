## Top 3 fixable UI/product issues

1. **Deposits & payouts — "paid in DoorLoop, but not in my bank"** — 93 escalations (15%), CX 64.1%.
   Payments show as paid/deposited in DoorLoop but the money isn't in the customer's bank, or
   amounts don't match. Pain points: payout visibility, bank reconciliation (uncleared deposits,
   unmatchable bundled deposits), and confusing security-deposit recording.
   *Examples (customer quotes):*
   - "Why was only $885 transferred when my tenant paid $1,250?" (CX 1)
   - "Where is the money that shows as deposited but is not in my bank account?" (CX 3)
   - "It shows paid on June 12th, but the money hasn't appeared after 5 days." (CX 2)
   - "Why do deposits show as uncleared on reconciliation but don't appear under bank transactions?" (CX 4)
   - "Why does the deposit tab show a negative number when I withhold the deposit?" (CX 5)

2. **Leases — renewals, e-signature, and moving balances** — 84 escalations (13%), CX 65.5%.
   Multi-step lease workflows that block users mid-task: renewal errors ("dates conflict",
   auto-sent offers, leases not appearing in the Renewals queue), the e-signature/document flow
   (can't upload an existing signed lease, missing signature field, no Save button on templates),
   and moving tenants / transferring balances between leases.
   *Examples (customer quotes):*
   - "Why is there no save button when I finish creating a lease template?" (CX 2)
   - "Why can I only create a new lease and not upload an existing signed lease for signature?" (CX 2)
   - "Why can't the tenant see the signature field on the renewal lease?" (CX 4)
   - "How do I resolve the 'dates conflict' error when renewing my lease?" (CX 3)
   - "How can I transfer the balance from the new lease back to the old lease?" (CX 5)

3. **Merchant accounts — Stripe verification & setup** — 40 escalations (6%), CX 53.8%.
   Two sub-issues dominate: Stripe account access & verification (18) and merchant account setup
   & application (17). Users get stuck onboarding to payments — verification loops on
   already-verified accounts, re-verification from address mismatches, and "silent failure"
   card payments that leave no record.
   *Examples (customer quotes):*
   - "Why am I still getting 'verification incomplete' emails when my accounts are active and verified?" (CX 5)
   - "Why am I being asked to re-verify all my merchant accounts due to an address issue?" (CX 2)
   - "How can I verify my bank account if Stripe sends codes to my old, deactivated email and phone?" (CX 3)
   - "Card payments show 'in progress' to the tenant but leave no record — silent failures." (CX 4)
   - "Why can't I complete Stripe verification due to the questions asked about my account?" (CX 3)

## Cross-cutting root cause

The in-progress **migration to a new Stripe payment-processing structure** (which adds a
high-risk holding period) is feeding both Deposits and Merchant accounts escalations: during the
hold, DoorLoop shows the full pre-hold amount while the bank receives less, so the app's numbers
don't match the bank. Worth aligning Product, Payments, and CX on in-app transparency while the
migration completes.
